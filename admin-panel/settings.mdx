# Settings Configuration

The Settings page allows you to manage your company information, account details, and business preferences. This guide covers all available settings.

## Accessing Settings

Navigate to Settings from the admin dashboard sidebar. The Settings tab is available to developers and managers (with limited access).

## Company Profile

### Basic Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Company Settings">
    In the Settings tab, locate the "Company Information" section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Company Name">
    Enter or update your company name. This will be displayed in:
    - Project listings
    - Invoices
    - Partner program
    - Email communications
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click "Save" to update your company information. Changes are applied immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Detailed Company Information

For complete company profile, you can configure:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Contact Information">
    - Company name
    - Email address
    - Phone number
    - Physical address
  </Card>
  <Card title="Business Details">
    - Tax ID/VAT number
    - Bank information
    - IBAN (for invoicing)
    - Currency preference
    - VAT payer status
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Account Settings

### User Profile

Manage your personal account information:

- **Email Address**: Your login email (cannot be changed)
- **Password**: Update your password for security
- **Language Preference**: Set your default interface language
- **Notification Settings**: Configure email notifications

### Security

<Tip>
Keep your account secure:
- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication if available
- Don't share your login credentials
- Log out when using shared devices
</Tip>

## Subscription Management

View and manage your subscription from the Settings tab:

- **Current Plan**: See your active subscription details
- **Plan Features**: View included features for your plan
- **Billing History**: Access invoices and payment records
- **Upgrade/Downgrade**: Change your plan as needed

<Info>
Subscription management is available in a dedicated Subscription tab. See [Subscription Management](/subscriptions/managing) for details.
</Info>

## Manager Accounts

### Adding Managers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Manager Accounts">
    In Settings, go to the "Manager Accounts" section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add New Manager">
    Click "Add Manager" and enter:
    - Manager email address
    - Manager name
    - Assign to workspace (optional)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Access">
    Set project access permissions:
    - All projects
    - Specific projects
    - Project groups
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send Invitation">
    Click "Invite" to send an invitation email to the manager.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Existing Managers

For existing managers, you can:
- Edit access permissions
- Reassign to different workspaces
- Remove manager access
- View manager activity

<Warning>
Removing a manager will revoke all their access immediately. They will lose access to all assigned projects and leads.
</Warning>

## Notification Preferences

Configure how you receive notifications:

- **Email Notifications**: Receive updates via email
- **Lead Alerts**: Get notified of new leads

- **Subscription Reminders**: Payment and renewal reminders

## Troubleshooting Settings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Changes not saving">
    - Ensure you clicked "Save" button
    - Check for validation errors (red highlights)
    - Refresh the page and try again
    - Clear browser cache if issues persist
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't access certain settings">
    - Verify your user role (developer vs manager)
    - Check workspace permissions
    - Contact support if you believe you should have access
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Manager invitation not received">
    - Check spam/junk folder
    - Verify email address is correct
    - Resend invitation from manager accounts section
    - Contact support if issue continues
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
