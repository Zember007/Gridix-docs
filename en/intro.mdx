## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register your account">
    Sign up for a Gridix account using your email address. You'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.
    
    <Note>
    New accounts start with a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan, giving you access to all premium features.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create your first project">
    Create a new project by importing data from Excel/Google Sheets or setting it up manually. Configure basic project information like name, address, and building details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload floor plans">
    Upload floor plan images for each floor in your building. Gridix will help you mark apartments and configure their properties.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure apartments">
    Set up apartment details including numbers, rooms, area, price, and status. Add custom fields to match your business needs.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Embed on your website">
    Generate the widget code and embed it on your website. Your interactive floor plan is now live and ready to collect leads!
  </Step>
</Steps>
