## Getting Started

### Join the Program

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register as Partner">
    Click "Become a Partner" in the admin dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Partner Type">
    Select your partner type:
    - Basic Partner (20% commission)
    - Integrator Partner (40-50% commission)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Get Your Links">
    Receive your partner referral links and codes.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Earning">
    Share your links and start earning commissions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Referral Links

Generate and manage:

- **Partner Code**: Unique partner identifier
- **Referral Link**: Shareable link for clients
- **Custom Links**: Create project-specific links

## Commission Structure

### Basic Partner

- **Commission**: 20% of subscription fees
- **Requirements**: Share referral link
- **No Implementation**: No involvement in setup needed
- **Quick Start**: Can start in 5 minutes

### Integrator Partner

- **Commission**: 40% (up to 10 clients) or 50% (10+ clients)
- **Requirements**: Create accounts and provide support
- **Implementation**: Full client implementation
- **Recommended Status**: Get Gridix recommended status
