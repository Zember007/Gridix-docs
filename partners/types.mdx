# Partner Types

Gridix offers two partner types to suit different business models. Choose the format that works best for your business.

## Basic Partner

### Overview

Basic Partner is perfect for marketers, agents, and consultants who want to earn without implementation involvement.

<Card title="Basic Partner" icon="user">
- **Commission**: 20% from each paid project
- **Effort**: Minimal - just share your link
- **Implementation**: No involvement required
- **Start Time**: 5 minutes to get started
</Card>

### How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Partner Link">
    Receive your unique referral link after registration.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Share with Clients">
    Share your partner link with potential clients (developers).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Client Signs Up">
    Client registers through your link and subscribes.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Earn Commission">
    Earn 20% from each paid subscription while projects are active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Benefits

- **No Technical Knowledge**: No technical skills required
- **Passive Income**: Earn while projects remain active
- **Easy to Start**: Can begin in 5 minutes
- **Access to Statistics**: Track performance and earnings

### Requirements

- Share your partner referral link
- No implementation involvement
- No technical knowledge needed
- Marketing and sales focus

## Integrator Partner

### Overview

Integrator Partner is recommended for digital agencies, CRM integrators, and freelancers who want higher commissions through full implementation.

<Card title="Integrator Partner" icon="settings">
- **Commission**: 40% (up to 10 clients) or 50% (10+ clients)
- **Effort**: Full implementation and support
- **Implementation**: Create accounts and provide support
- **Recommended**: Get Gridix recommended status
</Card>

### How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register as Integrator">
    Sign up as Integrator Partner (requires approval for 40-50% level).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Client Accounts">
    Set up Gridix accounts for your clients.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide Implementation">
    Implement Gridix for clients:
    - Project setup
    - Floor plan configuration
    - CRM integration
    - Customization
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manage Clients">
    Provide ongoing support and management.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Earn Higher Commission">
    Earn 40-50% commission based on client count.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Commission Tiers

- **Up to 10 Clients**: 40% commission
- **10+ Clients**: 50% commission
- **Recommended Status**: After 5-10 successful implementations

### Benefits

- **Higher Earnings**: 40-50% vs 20% commission
- **Recommended Status**: Get Gridix recommended partner status
- **Account Management**: Independently manage client accounts
- **Commission on Top-ups**: Earn from client account top-ups

### Requirements

- Actively implement and manage clients
- Provide client support
- Technical knowledge helpful
- After 5-10 projects, move to 40-50% commission level

## Comparison

| Feature | Basic Partner | Integrator Partner |
|---------|---------------|-------------------|
| Commission | 20% | 40-50% |
| Implementation Required | No | Yes |
| Client Support | No | Yes |
| Technical Knowledge | Not needed | Helpful |
| Start Time | 5 minutes | Varies |
| Recommended Status | No | Yes (after 5-10 clients) |
| Best For | Marketers, Agents | Agencies, Integrators |

## Choosing the Right Type

### Choose Basic Partner If

- You're a marketer or consultant
- You have contacts with developers
- You want passive income
- You don't want implementation involvement
- You want to start quickly

### Choose Integrator Partner If

- You're a digital agency or freelancer
- You can provide implementation services
- You want higher commissions
- You're comfortable with technical setup
- You want to build long-term client relationships

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I become a partner?">
    Click 'Become a Partner', register — the link is available immediately. Choose a format: simple referral (20%) or full implementation (40-50%).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="When will I receive my first payout?">
    After the client pays. Minimum withdrawal is $100, processing time up to 7 days. You can see all data in your account in real-time.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What do I need to become an integrator?">
    Actively implement and manage clients. After 5–10 projects, you move to the 40–50% commission level with full team support.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I just share a link?">
    Yes, this is the basic partnership format with 20% commission. Perfect for marketers and consultants who have contacts with developers.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
