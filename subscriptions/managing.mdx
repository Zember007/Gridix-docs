# Managing Your Subscription

This guide covers all aspects of managing your Gridix subscription, from viewing your current plan to updating payment methods.

## Accessing Subscription Settings

### Navigation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Admin Dashboard">
    Log in to your Gridix account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Go to Subscription">
    Click "Subscription" in the sidebar navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Subscription">
    See your current subscription details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Subscription Details

### Current Plan Information

The subscription page displays:

- **Current Plan**: Your active plan (Basic or Pro)
- **Status**: Active, expired, or cancelled
- **Expiration Date**: When your subscription expires
- **Features**: List of included features
- **Billing Cycle**: Monthly, quarterly, or annual

### Subscription Status

Status indicators:

- **Active**: Subscription is active and working
- **Trial**: Currently in free trial period
- **Expired**: Subscription has expired
- **Cancelled**: Subscription cancelled but still active until period ends

## Upgrading Your Plan

### Upgrade to Pro

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Available Plans">
    See Pro plan details and pricing.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Duration">
    Choose subscription duration:
    - 1 month
    - 3 months (5% discount)
    - 6 months (10% discount)
    - 1 year (20% discount)
    - 2 years (30% discount)
    - 3 years (50% discount)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Pricing">
    See total price with discount applied.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Enter payment information and complete upgrade.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Activation">
    Pro features are activated immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Prorated Billing

<Info>
When upgrading mid-cycle, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remaining period. The difference between plans is calculated and charged.
</Info>

## Downgrading Your Plan

### Switch to Basic

<Steps>
  <Step title="Cancel Pro Subscription">
    Go to subscription settings and click "Cancel Subscription" or "Downgrade".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Downgrade">
    Confirm you want to downgrade to Basic plan.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Continue Until End">
    Pro features remain available until current billing period ends.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Downgrade">
    Account automatically switches to Basic plan after expiration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Downgrading will remove access to Pro features like CRM integration and custom domains. Make sure to export any data you need before downgrading.
</Warning>

## Renewal Settings

### Automatic Renewal

<Check>
By default, subscriptions renew automatically:
- No action needed
- Continuous service
- Payment charged automatically
- Email notification before renewal
</Check>

### Disabling Auto-Renewal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Subscription Settings">
    Go to subscription management.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Disable Auto-Renewal">
    Toggle off "Auto-Renew" option.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm disabling auto-renewal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Disabling auto-renewal means your subscription will expire at the end of the current period. You'll need to manually renew.
</Warning>

## Subscription Expiration

### Expired Subscription

When subscription expires:

- **Access to Pro Features**: Removed
- **Account Status**: Switches to Basic plan
- **Data Preservation**: All projects and data remain
- **Reactivation**: Upgrade to Pro anytime to restore features

### Renewing Expired Subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Expired Status">
    See expiration notice in subscription tab.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Plan">
    Select plan and duration to renew.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Process payment to reactivate subscription.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Trial Period

### Free Trial Management

During 14-day free trial:

- **Full Pro Access**: All Pro features available
- **Trial Duration**: 14 days from account creation
- **No Charge**: Completely free
- **Conversion**: Auto-converts to Basic if not upgraded

### Trial Expiration

When trial expires:

- **Automatic Conversion**: Account switches to Basic plan
- **Pro Features**: Removed
- **Data Safe**: All projects and data preserved
- **Upgrade Anytime**: Can upgrade to Pro later

## Billing Cycles

### Available Cycles

- **Monthly**: Billed every month
- **Quarterly**: Billed every 3 months (5% discount)
- **Semi-Annual**: Billed every 6 months (10% discount)
- **Annual**: Billed yearly (20% discount)
- **2 Years**: Billed every 2 years (30% discount)
- **3 Years**: Billed every 3 years (50% discount)

### Changing Billing Cycle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Wait for Renewal">
    Billing cycle can be changed at renewal time.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select New Cycle">
    Choose new billing cycle during renewal.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply Discount">
    Discount is automatically applied for longer cycles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment failed">
    - Check payment method is valid
    - Verify sufficient funds
    - Update payment method
    - Contact support if issue persists
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Subscription not activating">
    - Wait a few minutes for processing
    - Refresh the page
    - Check payment confirmation
    - Contact support with payment details
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't access Pro features">
    - Verify subscription is active
    - Check subscription status
    - Refresh browser cache
    - Contact support for assistance
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
