# Subscription Plans

Gridix offers flexible subscription plans to suit different needs. Choose the plan that works best for your business.

## Available Plans

### Basic Plan

<Card title="Pro Plan" icon="crown">

## Pricing Structure

### Monthly Pricing

- **Basic Plan**: Affordable monthly pricing
- **Pro Plan**: Higher tier with advanced features

### Subscription Duration Discounts

Save more with longer subscriptions:

- **3 Months**: 5% discount
- **6 Months**: 10% discount
- **1 Year**: 20% discount
- **2 Years**: 30% discount
- **3 Years**: 50% discount

<Tip>
Longer subscription periods offer significant savings. Consider annual plans for best value.
</Tip>

## Choosing the Right Plan

### When to Choose Basic

Basic plan is ideal for:
- Small developers
- Testing the platform
- Basic floor plan needs
- Limited CRM requirements

### When to Choose Pro

Pro plan is recommended for:
- Professional developers
- Need for CRM integration
- Custom branding requirements
- Priority support needs
- Multiple projects

## Plan Features in Detail

### Basic Plan Features

- **Unlimited Projects**: Create as many projects as needed
- **Floor Plans**: Upload and manage floor plans
- **Apartment Management**: Full apartment configuration
- **Lead Collection**: Collect leads from widgets
- **Basic Widgets**: Embed floor plans on websites
- **Email Support**: Standard email support

### Pro Plan Features

All Basic features plus:

- **CRM Integration**: Connect AmoCRM for automatic lead processing
- **Custom Domains**: Use your own domain for projects
- **Advanced Templates**: Professional templates and layouts
- **Priority Support**: Faster response times
- **Analytics**: Advanced analytics and reporting
- **API Access**: Programmatic access (if available)

## Subscription Management

### Viewing Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Subscription">
    Go to "Subscription" tab in admin dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Current Plan">
    See your active plan and features.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Status">
    View subscription status and expiration date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Upgrading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Pro Plan">
    Choose Pro plan from available options.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Duration">
    Select subscription duration (1 month, 3 months, etc.).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Complete payment to activate Pro plan.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Downgrading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Cancel Pro Subscription">
    Cancel Pro plan from subscription settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Continue Until End">
    Pro features available until subscription period ends.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Downgrade">
    Account downgrades to Basic plan automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What does the free trial include?">
    The 14-day trial includes all Pro plan features: CRM integration, custom domains, advanced templates, and priority support. No credit card required.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I change my plan?">
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the difference will be prorated for the remaining period.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do discounts work?">
    The longer the subscription period, the bigger the discount: 3 months - 5%, 6 months - 10%, 1 year - 20%, 2 years - 30%, 3 years - 50%. Discounts are applied automatically.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens when I cancel?">
    When you cancel your subscription, you retain access until the end of the paid period. After that, your account is switched to the Basic plan with limited features.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
